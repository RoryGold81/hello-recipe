Instagram Caption Fetcher

Enter an Instagram post URL to fetch its caption using oEmbed.

Instructions:

  1. Find an Instagram post with a recipe or content you want to extract.
  2. Copy the URL of that post.
  3. Paste the URL in the field above and click "Fetch Caption".
  4. The app will attempt to extract the caption using Instagram's oEmbed API.

Note: This only works with public Instagram posts.
